Travel Tips & Guides

Consejos útiles para hacer tu viaje perfecto

Best Time to Visit

Tropical Destinations

December - April: Dry season, perfect weather. May - November: Rainy season, lower prices.

European Cities

April - June, September - October: Mild weather, fewer crowds. July - August: Peak season, warmer.

Mountain Regions

June - September: Best for hiking. December - March: Ski season.

What to Pack

Beach Destinations

  • Swimwear and cover-ups
  • Sunscreen (SPF 50+)
  • Sun hat and sunglasses
  • Light, breathable clothing
  • Waterproof bag for electronics

Mountain/Cooler Climates

  • Layered clothing
  • Comfortable hiking shoes
  • Warm jacket or fleece
  • Rain jacket
  • Backpack for day trips

City Destinations

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Versatile, mix-and-match outfits
  • Light jacket or cardigan
  • Small crossbody bag
  • Power adapter for electronics

Transportation Tips

  • Book in advance: Flights and accommodations are often cheaper when booked 2-3 months ahead.
  • Local transportation: Research public transport options. Many cities offer day passes for tourists.
  • Rideshare apps: Download local rideshare apps before arriving (Uber, Grab, etc.).
  • Walking tours: Many cities offer free walking tours - great way to orient yourself.
  • Rental cars: Check if you need an international driving permit for your destination.

Safety Tips

  • Sun protection: Always wear sunscreen, even on cloudy days. Reapply every 2 hours.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water, especially in hot climates or at high altitudes.
  • Comfortable footwear: Wear appropriate shoes for the terrain to avoid injuries.
  • Travel insurance: Consider purchasing travel insurance for medical emergencies and trip cancellations.
  • Keep copies: Have digital copies of important documents (passport, ID, insurance) stored securely.
  • Emergency contacts: Save local emergency numbers and your country's embassy contact info.

Local Rules & Important Customs

  • Dress codes: Some religious sites require modest clothing. Always check before visiting.
  • Tipping culture: Research tipping customs - they vary greatly by country.
  • Photography: Some places prohibit photography. Always ask permission, especially in sacred sites.
  • Bargaining: In many markets, bargaining is expected. Start at 50-60% of the asking price.
  • Respect local customs: Learn basic greetings and customs. A little effort goes a long way.

Climate Information

Tropical

Hot and humid year-round. Two seasons: dry (Dec-Apr) and rainy (May-Nov). Average: 25-30°C.

Temperate

Four distinct seasons. Mild summers (20-25°C) and cool winters (5-15°C). Best: Spring and Fall.

Mediterranean

Hot, dry summers (25-35°C) and mild, wet winters (10-15°C). Perfect: May-June, September-October.

Mountain

Cooler temperatures, significant day/night temperature variations. Can drop below freezing at night.
